What is Soccer

Football also called Soccer is a game in which two teams of 11 players,
using any part of their bodies except their hands and arms, try to move or
in other words dribble, pass, or shoot the ball into the opposing teams goal.
Only the goalkeeper is permitted to handle the ball and may do so only within
the penalty area surrounding the goal. The team that scores more goals wins.
