What is Volleyball

Volleyball, game played by two teams, usually of six players on a side,
in which the players use their hands to bat a ball back and forth over a
high net, trying to make the ball touch the court within the opponents
playing area before it can be returned. To prevent this a player on the
opposing team bats the ball up and toward a teammate before it touches the
court surfaceâ€”that teammate may then volley it back across the net or bat
it to a third teammate who volleys it across the net. A team is allowed only
three touches of the ball before it must be returned over the net.
